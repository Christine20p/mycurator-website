Services

Property presentation services by category

The final scope is confirmed after inspection so each property receives the right treatment plan, preparation sequence, and presentation finish.

Residential

Residential presentation services

Prepared for listing photography, open homes, private viewings, and weekly or monthly presentation upkeep while the property is on market.

Property Presentation Cleaning

Routine presentation cleaning covers floors, skirtings, kitchens, bathrooms, glazing touch-ups, dust removal, and final room reset before viewings.

Premium Deep Presentation Clean

Deep cleaning targets buildup in grout, cupboards, fixtures, trims, appliances, and overlooked corners to lift the overall standard before premium marketing activity.

Home Staging Setup

Furniture, decor, soft finishes, and focal points are arranged to improve flow, scale, lighting, and buyer perception throughout the home.

Property Pest Protection

Risk areas are inspected and treated to reduce visible pest activity, protect finishes, and maintain a clean presentation standard.

Odour Neutralisation Treatment

Odour sources are identified, treated, and neutralised through safe deodorising methods so the space smells fresh rather than artificially masked.

Interior Repainting & Refresh

Walls, ceilings, trims, and selected surfaces are patched, prepared, and repainted where needed to restore a clean and consistent finish.

Pool Presentation Service

The pool area is cleaned, visually reset, and presentation-checked so it reads as a maintained lifestyle feature during showings.

Lawn Presentation Maintenance

Grass is cut, edged, and tidied to create a neat frontage and preserve consistent curb appeal across the listing period.

Landscape Trimming

Shrubs, hedges, and planting lines are shaped back to improve sightlines, access routes, and the overall balance of the exterior presentation.

Lawn Colour Restoration

Patchy or faded lawn areas are improved with presentation-safe colour enhancement and surface preparation for a healthier visual finish.

Interior Presentation Repairs

Minor wear items such as scuffs, chips, loose fittings, sealant gaps, and cosmetic defects are corrected to improve the finished look.

Driveway & Pavement Restoration

External hard surfaces are pressure cleaned and stain-treated to remove grime, algae, and dull presentation from the approach to the home.

Office

Office presentation services

Designed for client-facing offices, reception areas, workspaces, boardrooms, and surrounding grounds that need to present with clarity and order.

Office Presentation Cleaning

Reception areas, workstations, floors, glass, kitchens, and restrooms are cleaned and reset to maintain a polished day-to-day presentation.

Premium Office Deep Clean

Detailed cleaning is carried out across joinery, fixtures, vents, glazing, shared spaces, and buildup-prone surfaces before high-value meetings or inspections.

Office Staging Setup

Reception styling, meeting-room layouts, decor placement, and presentation detailing are arranged to support brand perception and client confidence.

Workplace Pest Protection

Problem areas are assessed and treated to prevent visible pest issues in kitchens, storage areas, ceilings, and client-facing spaces.

Odour Neutralisation Treatment

Air quality and source odours are addressed across shared office zones to create a cleaner, more professional environment for staff and visitors.

Office Interior Repainting

Selected office walls, trims, and visible finishes are refreshed to improve brightness, uniformity, and overall presentation quality.

Exterior Presentation Maintenance

Entrances, facade touchpoints, approach areas, and external presentation elements are maintained to support a consistent first impression.

Landscape Maintenance

Soft landscaping around office grounds is maintained through mowing, edging, cleanup, and visual reset of planted areas.

Landscape Trimming

Hedges, screening plants, and boundary vegetation are trimmed back to keep access points, signage, and sightlines clear.

Lawn Colour Restoration

Faded lawn areas are treated for better visual consistency so the grounds present as maintained during visits and inspections.

Interior Wear & Tear Restoration

Minor cosmetic damage, scuffs, chips, and visible defects are repaired to reduce distraction in office interiors.

Pavement & Walkway Cleaning

Entrance paving and office walkways are pressure cleaned and reset to improve the journey from parking or gate access to the front door.

Commercial

Commercial presentation services

Structured for customer-facing, industrial, mixed-use, and operational commercial spaces that need a stronger market-ready appearance.

Commercial Presentation Cleaning

Customer zones, circulation areas, restrooms, glazing, floors, and visible touchpoints are cleaned to elevate the commercial environment.

Premium Commercial Deep Clean

Heavy-duty buildup, neglected corners, equipment-adjacent surfaces, and hard-to-reach areas are tackled to improve the overall finish.

Commercial Space Staging

Reception zones, waiting areas, display points, and selected interiors are arranged to improve how the space is read during presentations.

Commercial Pest Protection

High-risk operational and exterior areas are inspected and treated to reduce pest visibility and protect presentation standards.

Odour Neutralisation Treatment

Commercial odours from storage, traffic, dampness, or operational use are treated at source to improve the visitor experience.

Interior Repainting & Refresh

Selected commercial interiors are patched and repainted where fading, scuffing, or uneven finishes weaken the presentation.

Exterior Property Maintenance

Exterior touchpoints, frontage, visible structures, and client-facing approach areas are maintained to improve curbside credibility.

Landscape Maintenance

Commercial grounds are cut back, cleaned, and reset to support a controlled and well-managed appearance across the site.

Landscape Trimming

Boundary planting, screening, and overgrowth are trimmed to reveal access routes, signage, and more ordered site lines.

Lawn Colour Restoration

Lawned commercial areas are visually refreshed to reduce patchiness and improve uniformity for marketing and inspections.

Interior Wear & Tear Restoration

Visible cosmetic defects are corrected so the space feels better maintained and more ready for evaluation or occupancy interest.

Pavement & Driveway Restoration

Drive routes, loading approaches, and front-of-site hard surfaces are cleaned and restored to improve first impressions from arrival.

Vacant Land

Vacant land presentation services

Used where access, boundary clarity, and site legibility need to improve before viewings, consultations, or investor presentations.

Vegetation Clearing

Overgrowth, long grass, brush, and view-blocking vegetation are cleared back to improve visibility and access across the site.

Land Preparation

Site approach areas are prepared through selective clearing, access opening, and practical ground preparation aligned to inspection findings.

Weed Removal & Site Clearing

Invasive weeds, dumped material, and loose site debris are removed to create a cleaner, more readable land parcel for presentation.

Vacant Land Presentation

Scheduled through consultation and site inspection. The scope may include line-of-sight improvement, boundary emphasis, access path definition, and cleanup sequencing.
